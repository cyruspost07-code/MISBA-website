About Us

Our mission

We help Bulldogs explore careers at the intersection of business, data, and technology—through workshops, employer connections, and hands-on projects you can put on your resume.

What MIS & Business Analytics majors do

MIS focuses on how organizations use technology and information systems to solve problems. Business Analytics emphasizes data, modeling, and visualization to support decisions. Both paths blend communication, technical fluency, and business judgment—skills recruiters actively seek.

Why join

  • Meet peers, alumni, and employers in a supportive club environment
  • Practice tools like SQL, spreadsheets, BI platforms, and presentation skills
  • Highlight projects and leadership on your resume and LinkedIn
